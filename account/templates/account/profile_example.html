How It Works

A Basic Profile has the following info:

  • Name, Rank, Hometown, etc
  • Dates Assigned (Team & Army)
  • Profile Image

Additional (optional) info:

  • Jobs & Highlights
  • Awards & Badges
  • Licenses & Ratings
  • Stories
  • More Images (up to 12)

Click the button below to update your Profile. Or keep reading to learn more.

Update My Profile Info

Basic Info: This is where you update your Name, Rank, Nicknames, Hometown, MOS, License Number and GKAS Year.

You even have the option of using one image for your Profile and a different image for the Roster. The Roster is where people search for you on the website.

Team and Army Dates: The dates you were assigned to the Team and the dates you were in the Army.

Profile Picture: The Image you want shown on the website. A lot of members use their "Hero Shot".

Additional Information you can add

Jobs & Highlights: Jobs you held and career highlights you want to share.

Individual Categories are:

  • Team Jobs & Highlights
  • Army Jobs & Highlights
  • Civilian Jobs & Highlights

Awards & Badges

Licenses & Ratings

Current Status: What are you up to these days?

Public Comment: Shown next to your image on the website. Most members say something about their time on the team.

Stories: Do you have a story you want to tell? This is the place to add it. Add as many as you would like.

More Images: Add up to 12 images. Most members add pictures from their time on the team.
